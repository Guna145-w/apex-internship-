# рџ“– Task 4 вЂ“ Data Storytelling & Statistical Validation

## Objective

Transform analytical findings into a compelling business story and validate insights using statistical methods.

### Business Story

The analysis focused on understanding customer purchasing behavior, sales performance, and operational trends to help improve business decisions.

### Statistical Validation

Performed an **Independent Two-Sample T-Test** to compare customer spending between Cash and Card payment methods.

### Hypotheses

**Null Hypothesis (Hв‚Ђ)**

There is no significant difference in average spending between customers using Cash and Card payment methods.

**Alternative Hypothesis (Hв‚Ѓ)**

There is a significant difference in average spending between customers using Cash and Card payment methods.

### Outcome

The hypothesis test provided statistical evidence to support data-driven decision-making.

### Skills Learned

* Data Storytelling
* Statistical Analysis
* Hypothesis Testing
* Business Communication
